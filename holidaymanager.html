HolidayManager

Holidays are a scarce good and it is very important to manage then appropriately.

HolidayManager allows you to easily keep track of the days you have taken off from work and know, at a glance, the days you still have available.

 
Click on one of the cells

1.- Choose a date

2.- Write a note (optional)

3.- Select Annotate, Used or Cancel that date
Select a date on the calendar
 
In Preferences, you can change the total number of days per year (number of days) and/or year
